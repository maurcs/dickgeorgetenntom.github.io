Short Circuit Traveling Film Festival At MSU Riley Center Save Email Print
Posted: 6:48 PM Oct 14, 2007
 Last Updated: 11:45 PM Oct 14, 2007
 Reporter: Chris Brennaman
Email Address: chris.brennaman@wtok.com
A | A | A

The MSU Riley Center was the site of a film festival Sunday evening.

The Short Circuit Traveling Film Festival was presented in the studio theater. Twelve short films were shown, including Dick-George, Tenn-Tom, A Documentary about the rivalry between President Richard Nixon and Alabama Governor George Wallace in the 1970's. The Director of the film was happy to have his film on display at the Riley Center.
