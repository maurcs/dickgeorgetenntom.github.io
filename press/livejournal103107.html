HSDF 23/24/27

  • Oct. 27th, 2007 at 9:47 PM

More films:


Freeheld - excellent - dying New Jersey police officer fights to transfer her pension benefits to her partner

Out Of the South - good - six gay men in Los Angeles talk about leaving the Rural South

The Old Gray Lady: Arkansas' First Newspaper - good - history of the first newspaper in Arkansas, the Arkansas Gazette (eaten by its rival in 1991)

Gimme Green - average - biased look at the chemicals and efforts that are used to keep a suburban lawn

Trashed - average - shows you where the trash goes, surprise, it's a landfill

Middle Fork of the Saline River - bad - student production about a local river with biologists rambling on about the wildlife and plants

Watching the Waters Rise - bad - student production about the flood of 1927

King Corn - good to a point - two guys in their early 20's move to Iowa to grow an acre of corn. I was fine until the guy reached into the stomach of a live cow through an open wound.

Soldiers of Conscience - excellent - soldiers have always had an option of filing as a conscientious objector, a look at some of the Iraq War objectors

Runner's High - excellent - teenagers from Oakland, CA train and run the Los Angeles marathon

Film Class - excellent - filmmaker teaches Black Bedouin women filmmaking

Chain of Life - excellent - artisan who uses wood stamps (an almost extinct technique) to create a kimono - BONUS filmmaker was in attendance in a hand-stamped kimono

Talk to Me - bad - man makes a movie of photographs and messages from his answering machine

Boodog: How to Roast a Mongolian Marmot - bad - I had to close my eyes while a group of Mongolian men killed and cooked a marmot

Dick-George, Tenn-Tom - average - look at Nixon's visit to Alabama for the dedication of a waterway

In Your Purse: Archaeology of the American Hand Handbag - average - look at the contents of 100 purses

Motodrom - average - I had to close my eyes while motorcycles circled around a big barrel-shaped stadium thing

When the Light's Red - average - man questions giving money to panhandlers at intersections

The Fighting Cholitas - excellent - indigenous Indian women in Bolivia who jump into the wrestling ring every Sunday in their traditional multi-layered skirts

Hush - bad - film footage scooting forward and backward seconds at a time while a female narrator describes the drug/alcohol/sex abuse in her family
